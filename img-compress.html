How Our Image Compressor Works

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce image file sizes while maintaining visual quality. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = better quality)
  3. Select your desired output format (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  4. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  5. Download your optimized image

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free with no hidden charges.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. We don't upload or store your images.

What's the maximum file size I can compress?

The current limit is 10MB per image. For larger files, consider using desktop software.

Which format gives the best compression?

WebP typically offers the best compression with good quality. JPG is best for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency.
